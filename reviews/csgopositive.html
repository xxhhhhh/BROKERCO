Review of CSGOPositive

CSGOPositive is a trusted esports and MMA betting platform that has been operating since 2017. Designed with a user-friendly interface, it caters mainly to the CIS audience and focuses on popular titles such as CS2, Dota 2, League of Legends, and Valorant.

The platform supports a wide range of deposit and withdrawal options, including cryptocurrencies, e-wallets, and bank transfers. Most withdrawal methods are free of charge, except for in-game items and card payouts, which may carry fees — offering cost-efficiency for regular users.

Although the number of supported esports disciplines is somewhat limited, the site offers solid match coverage and a consistently high player base, often exceeding 800 online users.

A standout feature of CSGOPositive is its cashback system: users earn "crystals" that can be converted into real money. Cashback rates increase as users level up. Additionally, a roulette mode adds an extra gambling element to the platform.

Overall, CSGOPositive is a reliable and convenient choice for esports betting. With fair policies, a smooth interface, flexible payment options, and useful bonus systems, it appeals especially to players focused on CS2 and other major titles.

Trust

CSGOPositive has been operating since 2017 and holds a license issued by the government of Anjouan (Comoros Islands), confirming basic legitimacy. The platform has implemented Provably Fair for some games and demonstrates moderate operational transparency. However, the anonymity of the owners, limited public information, and isolated incidents — such as the blocking of a large win in 2022 — hold it back from a top rating. Nevertheless, the site maintains a reputation for fairness, especially within the Russian-speaking community, and continues to grow actively.

Support

Customer support at CSGOPositive is available via ticket system and email. Average response time is 4–6 hours, and up to 24 hours on weekends. A knowledge base on the website covers most common topics, and an active community chat allows users to get peer advice. The absence of live chat makes urgent issue resolution slower, but the overall support quality is respectable.

Payments

The platform supports multiple deposit methods: cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, USDT), e-wallets, and bank cards, though most come with transaction fees. The minimum withdrawal amount ranges from $5 to $10, depending on the method, and processing times can take up to 3 business days. While instant withdrawals via skins are available, overall financial flexibility is behind that of competitors.

Playability

CSGOPositive focuses primarily on betting for esports and MMA. Popular games such as CS2, Dota 2, Valorant, and League of Legends are supported, with options for single bets and accumulators. The platform also features roulette and a Defuse mode (similar to Mines) with Provably Fair. While game diversity and depth are limited compared to larger gambling platforms, features like crystal cashback, tournaments, and a progressive leveling system help keep the gameplay engaging for regular users.
