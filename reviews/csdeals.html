Review of CS.Deals

CS.Deals is a marketplace for buying and selling in-game items from CS2, Rust, Dota 2, and Team Fortress 2. Operating since 2016, the platform has earned a strong reputation, attracting over 125,000 monthly visitors. It supports 12 languages, making it accessible to a global user base.

The interface is clean and user-friendly, and the search tool helps users quickly locate specific skins. Compared to other CS2 skin marketplaces, CS.Deals offers relatively low fees, which is a notable advantage. However, payment and withdrawal options are limited — users can use USD/EUR cards and Bitcoin only.

KYC verification is required to withdraw funds to real money, adding a layer of security but potentially creating inconvenience for some. Customer support is provided via email ticketing, which may result in slower response times compared to live chat systems.

Overall, CS.Deals is a trustworthy and efficient marketplace. While the platform has some limitations in payment methods and support speed, its solid reputation and low fees make it a reliable option for traders and casual users alike.

Trust

CS.Deals has been operating since 2017 and has become a well-known and stable platform among CS2 and other game traders. The site provides a high level of security, uses encrypted connections, and holds a positive reputation in the community. Stable performance, a lack of scandals, and a long-standing presence in the market confirm its reliability. Despite isolated incidents involving trade bots, the platform is generally considered safe and trustworthy.

Support

CS.Deals support operates via a ticket system, email, and Twitter messages. This approach may seem outdated compared to platforms offering live chat. Some users report long response times and limited language support. In non-standard cases (such as disputed transactions or item losses due to bans), resolving issues may take significant time, making support a weak point of the platform.

Payments

The platform supports a variety of deposit methods: cards (Visa, Mastercard), cryptocurrencies, PayPal, WebMoney, Trustly, and others. However, withdrawals are mostly limited to SEPA, bank transfers, and Bitcoin, some of which require KYC verification. Minimum amounts and limits vary by method. While crypto and bank transfers provide versatility, the lack of instant withdrawals and popular local payment options may reduce convenience for some users.

Pricing

One of CS.Deals' strongest points is its highly competitive pricing. The standard sale fee is only 2%, making the platform appealing to both new and experienced traders. Features like instant sales through bots, activity bonuses, and built-in price analysis tools further enhance its value. Compared to most competing marketplaces, CS.Deals offers excellent cost-to-feature balance.
