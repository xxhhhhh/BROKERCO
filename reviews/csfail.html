Trust

The platform uses Provably Fair technology and SSL encryption, and has passed RNG auditing by iTech Labs. Despite its technical advancements, users report occasional domain instability and controversial account suspensions, which slightly lowers the trust rating. However, in everyday use, the site performs reliably, especially for users in Eastern Europe and surrounding regions.

Support

Technical support is available via live chat, email, and social networks including Telegram and Discord. Most inquiries are answered within 2–5 minutes, though more complex issues — particularly those involving verification and withdrawals — are sometimes met with generic responses. Occasional failures in automated checks can also delay issue resolution. Overall, the support level is acceptable but would benefit from a more personalized approach.

Payments
