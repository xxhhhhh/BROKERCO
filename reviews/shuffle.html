Trust

Shuffle is a cryptocurrency gambling platform launched in 2023 and operating under a Curaçao Gaming Control Board license (OGL/2024/1337/0628). The platform employs Provably Fair technology to ensure game result transparency, and all connections are protected via SSL encryption. User accounts can be secured with two-factor authentication (2FA). However, due to the lack of external RNG audits and mandatory KYC verification even for minimum deposits, the trust level is considered moderate.

Support

Shuffle offers support via live chat, email, and official social media (Telegram, Discord, X). The most responsive channel is the 24/7 live chat, with replies typically arriving within 2–5 minutes. Email inquiries are handled within 24 hours. Users note the politeness of moderators and quick responses to standard questions. However, inquiries related to large transactions or verification may receive templated responses and lengthy follow-ups. Despite this, overall customer service is rated positively.

Payments

Shuffle’s financial system is crypto-based. Supported tokens include BTC, ETH, USDT, LTC, XMR, TRX, BNB, USDC, and others. Users can also purchase the native SHFL token via bank cards and Apple Pay through third-party gateways. Withdrawals are primarily in crypto and are processed within 5–60 minutes. Blockchain network conditions impact fees, which vary depending on load. KYC verification is mandatory to activate withdrawals, regardless of amount. Deposits via fiat cards require identity confirmation, which may be a barrier for new users. Nevertheless, the flexibility of crypto options makes the system convenient for experienced players.
