Trust

InsaneGG has been operating since 2020 and is managed by Gradient B.V. under a Curaçao license, providing a basic level of regulatory oversight. The platform uses a Provably Fair system, along with SSL encryption and two-factor authentication to protect user data and accounts. However, the Curaçao license and the lack of independent RNG audits limit transparency. Some users report account blocks and delays during large transactions, which makes the platform's trust level moderately positive.

Support

InsaneGG features a built-in live chat available 24/7. Average response time is 5–10 minutes, slightly slower than competitors. Still, support agents handle standard queries effectively. The platform actively engages with users through social media (Telegram, Discord, X), where updates and announcements are promptly shared. The on-site public chat helps newcomers understand the interface and get peer support quickly.

Payments

InsaneGG offers various deposit options: cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, LTC, USDT), bank cards, CS2 skins, and gift cards. Deposits are fast and include bonuses, such as a 100% first deposit bonus. Withdrawals are only possible via CS2 skins through Steam, which limits flexibility, especially for users preferring fiat or crypto. The withdrawal fee is up to 5%, and peak-hour delays are possible. The interface is intuitive but may take time to get used to due to the bonus system.

Playability

The platform features several popular game modes: Crash, Case Battles for PvP competition, and Upgrade for trading skins for higher-value ones. Animations are smooth, and the site's visual design is polished. Despite the limited number of modes, they are well-crafted and suitable for both new and experienced users. InsaneGG focuses on execution quality over quantity and successfully stands out in this regard.
