Trust

Rollbit has been operating since 2020 and has established itself as a recognizable crypto-gambling platform, especially due to its strong social media presence, collaboration with FaZe Clan, and involvement of popular streamers. However, from a regulatory perspective, there are risks: in 2024, its Curaçao license was revoked, and the company has applied for a new license in the Vanuatu jurisdiction, which is still pending. Despite the use of Provably Fair technology and basic security measures (SSL, 2FA), the absence of independent RNG audits and cases of account bans following large wins raise concerns. Transparency is high in original games, but overall trust in the platform remains moderate.

Support

Rollbit’s support is available via 24/7 live chat and email, with active presence on social platforms (Telegram, Discord, X). Average response time in chat is 2–5 minutes, and most standard queries are resolved quickly. However, complex issues—especially related to large withdrawals or KYC—often receive templated responses, with users reporting a lack of personalized assistance. Overall, the support level is solid, but not exceptional.

Deposit/Withdrawal

Rollbit works exclusively with cryptocurrencies and does not support fiat payments directly. Deposits can be made via BTC, ETH, LTC, and USDT, as well as through credit cards and Apple Pay — though these require third-party gateways and KYC. Minimum deposit amounts range from $5 to $20 depending on the method, and fees vary between 0.5% and 3.9%. Withdrawals are only available in crypto and typically processed within 5–60 minutes. KYC is required for large sums, especially over $10,000. Despite automation, about 18% of high-value withdrawals have experienced delays of 3 to 7 days, which experienced users should keep in mind.

Playability

Rollbit offers a wide range of gaming features: over 5,600 slots from top providers (Pragmatic Play, NetEnt, Hacksaw), live games from Evolution, original modes (Crash, Dice, Mines, Plinko), and betting on sports and esports (CS2, Dota 2, LoL, football, NBA, etc.). A standout feature is Duel Arena — fee-free PvP duels where users can wager NFTs or crypto. The platform also includes rakeback, daily tournaments, and a Rain System that drops free coins every 20 seconds for active users. The lack of mini-games isn’t felt due to the depth of the existing features and the platform’s own NFT and RLB token economy.
