Review of CSGO Market

CSGO Market is a peer-to-peer (P2P) marketplace for trading CS2 skins, launched in 2015. As one of the oldest operational platforms in the industry, it has built a strong reputation for reliability and transaction safety.

While the original site design may appear outdated, a newer version is available. Buying and selling items is quick and secure, typically taking only a few minutes. However, the interface may seem unintuitive for first-time users.

One of CSGO Market’s core strengths is its secure transaction system: sellers receive payment only after item delivery is confirmed. The platform is also known for reimbursing users in verified fraud cases, which has contributed to its positive image.

According to Trustpilot, CSGO Market holds a 4.8/5 rating based on over 1,400 reviews. The site receives around 2.9 million visits monthly, hosts over 16,500 unique items, and has processed more than $1 billion in total sales. It is operated by Theodotos Kapsis, with headquarters in Tallinn, Estonia.

In summary, CSGO Market is a trusted and secure platform for CS2 skin trading. Despite some visual limitations, its strong safety features and transaction volume make it a solid choice for both beginners and experienced traders.

Trust

CSGO Market has been operating since 2015 and is recognized as one of the oldest and most reliable P2P platforms for CS2 skin trading. The site has a high user rating (4.8/5 on Trustpilot) and has shown a willingness to compensate users in case of incidents, as it did after a 2022 breach. Despite some mixed reviews and a controversial reputation in certain circles, the platform is generally considered safe and has been operating stably for nearly a decade.

Support

CSGO Market’s support team is available via an on-site ticket system and email, though its effectiveness receives mixed feedback. Some users report long wait times or unresolved issues. However, in serious situations, the platform has previously responded quickly and compensated losses.

Payments

CSGO Market supports many deposit and withdrawal methods, including cards, cryptocurrencies, and local payment systems. Options include Bitcoin, Ethereum, Tether, SBP, YooMoney, and bank cards. Withdrawals incur an additional 3–5% fee depending on the method, with a maximum processing time of 3 business days — which aligns with market standards.

Pricing

The platform offers favorable trading conditions — the standard commission is only 5%. It also supports order functions, allowing users to buy at the best prices. Despite competition from other marketplaces, CSGO Market remains a strong player, especially in the CIS region.
