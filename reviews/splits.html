Trust

Splits.gg is a relatively new project operating in the Rust skin gambling space. The platform uses a Provably Fair system, provides basic technical protection, and supports a secure connection. However, the lack of a gambling license and limited transparency in several areas — including withdrawal terms and the user agreement — reduce its trust level. The platform still needs to prove itself as a consistently reliable service.

Support

Support on Splits.gg is available via live chat and email. The main contact channels operate 24/7 and are integrated into the site interface. Despite having basic functionality, the support system lacks depth: there are no additional contact options or a detailed help center with a ticketing system. Information on rules, withdrawals, and limits is also incomplete, requiring users to be cautious.

Payments

The platform supports deposits via Rust skins, cryptocurrencies, gift cards, and third-party services. Withdrawals are available in Rust skins and cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, LTC, USDT). Skin withdrawals are usually processed quickly, while crypto transactions may take more time. However, verification and payment policy lack sufficient transparency, which is important to consider when dealing with large amounts.

The visual design is minimalist, and while the interface is simple and clear, it still needs refinement.
