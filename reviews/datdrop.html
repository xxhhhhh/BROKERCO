Trust

DatDrop has been operating since 2017 and has established a strong presence among case opening platforms. The site uses a Provably Fair system based on SHA-2, allowing users to verify the fairness of each case opening. SSL encryption and Steam login provide basic account security. However, the lack of an official license and independent audits reduces overall transparency, and complaints about account bans after large wins raise concerns among some users.

Support

DatDrop support is limited to email. The average response time is up to 24 hours. There is no live chat, which limits the speed of solving urgent issues. The FAQ section helps with basic queries, but users may face templated or delayed responses in more complex situations.

Payments

DatDrop supports various deposit methods: cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, LTC), bank cards via G2A Pay, and CS2 skin deposits via Steam. Promo codes can also be used for a +5% deposit bonus. Withdrawals are only available via CS2 skins or Ethereum. A 5% fee applies to withdrawals, and most transactions are processed within minutes. The platform is tailored for users familiar with skin trading.
