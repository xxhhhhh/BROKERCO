Review of White.Market

White.Market is a peer-to-peer platform designed for trading CS2 skins. It is operated in collaboration with the cryptocurrency exchange WhiteBIT, lending credibility and enhanced security through its integration with a licensed crypto infrastructure.

The platform supports various payment options, including cryptocurrencies (with zero fees for Tether), bank transfers, and in-game items from CS2, Rust, and Dota 2 via the Skinsback service. This flexibility makes it suitable for both casual players and experienced traders.

White.Market actively engages with the gaming community by hosting events and promotions. Notably, its partnership with Faceit allows users to earn rewards on White.Market by completing gaming missions, adding a layer of gamified interaction.

In summary, White.Market is a reliable and secure skin trading platform with a user-friendly interface, competitive pricing, and community-focused features. Its P2P model and integration with crypto services make it a strong choice for users seeking efficient, commission-free trading options.

Trust

White.Market is backed by the reputation of the cryptocurrency exchange WhiteBIT, which manages the platform and ensures its legitimacy and baseline security. Thanks to partnerships with FACEIT, NAVI, and ESL, the marketplace is perceived as part of a large and recognizable ecosystem. However, the platform's relative newness, hidden ownership details, and lack of in-depth coverage in reputable sources leave some uncertainty, limiting the trust level to moderate.

Support

The platform offers technical support through a ticket system and FAQ. Despite claims of high transaction success and prompt issue resolution, users note a lack of personalized service and the absence of a full live chat. Support functions, but for a platform with such ambitions, a more robust and accessible support system would be expected.

Payments

White.Market supports deposits and withdrawals via cryptocurrencies (USDT, BTC, ETH), bank cards, and in-game items through the Skinsback service. One of the advantages is zero fees for deposits and withdrawals when using WhiteBIT and Tether. However, the required browser extension, mandatory WhiteBIT authorization, and geolocation restrictions may be inconvenient for some users.

Pricing

White.Market offers competitive prices on skins due to its direct user-to-user deal model and 0% fee for buyers. The 5% seller commission is average among competitors. Additional benefits include limit orders and custom stores, which expand users' trading capabilities.
