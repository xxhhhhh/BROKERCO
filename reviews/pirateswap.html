Review of PirateSwap

PirateSwap is a growing platform for trading and instant selling of CS2 skins, launched in 2020. Despite being relatively new, it has gained community trust due to transparent operations, ease of use, and a partnership with esports team MIBR.

Trades and sales are processed almost instantly: after logging in via Steam, users can select skins and complete deals within seconds. PirateSwap also offers new users up to $10 in exchange bonuses and a 35% deposit bonus when using a promo code.

The platform charges a 10% fee for instant selling, which is higher than average but justified by its speed and simplicity. Supported payment methods include bank cards, cryptocurrencies, Google Pay, and Skrill. Note: balance cannot be withdrawn directly — only through the instant sale function.

SSL encryption ensures user data security. Overall, PirateSwap is a convenient platform for fast skin trading and selling, though its limited withdrawal options and relatively high fee may not suit professional traders.

Trust

PirateSwap has been operating since 2020 and is rapidly gaining popularity among CS2 skin traders. It is officially operated by registered companies: MESSIDOR LTD (Cyprus) and ExitNode Limited (Hong Kong), confirming its legitimacy. A high Trustpilot rating (4.8 out of 5) underscores the platform’s reliability. A notable advantage is the partnership with the famous esports team MIBR. However, its relatively short operating history prevents assigning a maximum trust rating.

Support

PirateSwap support promptly responds to inquiries via online chat, as confirmed by numerous positive reviews. Issues are resolved quickly and professionally. During testing, the support team's quality and response time remained consistently high, meeting the expectations of experienced users.

Payments

PirateSwap provides a wide range of deposit methods, including bank cards, cryptocurrencies, and popular e-wallets. However, the major drawback is the inability to directly withdraw funds from the platform: balances can only be used for skin exchanges or purchases. Real money withdrawals are possible only through instant skin sales with a 10% commission, significantly limiting the convenience of financial operations.

Pricing

PirateSwap pricing is automated and depends on the rarity, wear, and availability of items. Despite the simplicity and speed of transactions, the commission for instant skin sales is 10%, noticeably higher than the market average (2–7%). This premium is justified by the convenience and immediacy of transactions but makes the platform less appealing to professional traders and users focused primarily on deal profitability.
