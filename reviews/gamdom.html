Review of Gamdom

Gamdom is a licensed crypto casino that began operations in 2016 as a skin gambling platform for CS2. Over time, it evolved into a full-fledged crypto-based casino while maintaining a strong community aspect.

The platform offers a wide range of games including slots, roulette, crash games, poker, and more. A key feature is its provably fair system, which allows players to verify the fairness of each outcome. A built-in chat enables users to interact and tip each other, fostering a lively social environment.

Gamdom supports major cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Litecoin, making deposits and withdrawals convenient. However, it no longer accepts CS2 skin deposits, which may disappoint longtime users who favored that format.

Overall, Gamdom is a reliable and innovative crypto casino offering fair games, fast transactions, and a vibrant community. Despite the discontinuation of skin support, it remains a top choice among crypto gamblers.

Trust

The platform uses a Provably Fair system to ensure fairness in all core games, and its RNG has been audited by the independent lab iTech Labs. Support for SSL encryption and two-factor authentication enhances the security of accounts and transactions. Thanks to its stability and transparency, Gamdom enjoys a high level of trust among players.

Support

Gamdom’s support operates 24/7 via live chat and email. Chat responses are generally quick, while email replies typically arrive within minutes to a couple of hours. The platform also actively uses social media to keep users informed, which helps resolve common questions and technical issues efficiently.

Payments
