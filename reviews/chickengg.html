However, the lack of independent audits and its recent market entry mean it can’t yet compete equally with more established platforms. Despite this, the platform shows strong intentions and stability.

Support

Chicken.GG support is available via ticket system and email, with average response times ranging from 2 to 12 hours. The team is also active on Discord, Telegram, and X (Twitter), posting updates and responding to inquiries. While there’s no live chat, users note that support responds quickly and is genuinely helpful. The on-site chat also enables fast peer-to-peer interaction.

Payments

Deposits are accepted via cryptocurrencies, bank cards, and skins (through a third-party service). Withdrawals are available only in cryptocurrency — BTC, ETH, LTC, USDT, and USDC.
