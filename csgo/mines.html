Mines (Minesweeper) - A popular gambling game mode inspired by the classic Minesweeper game, which has gained popularity on CS2 and CS:GO skin gambling sites. In this mode, players place bets with skins and open cells on the game board, trying to avoid mines. The goal of the game is to open as many safe cells as possible without hitting a mine. The more cells a player manages to open, the higher their winnings. If a player opens a cell with a mine, the game ends in a loss. The field is represented as a grid, the size of which varies: the larger the field, the higher the potential winnings, but the risk of hitting a mine also increases. Various sites offer their versions of the Mines mode with different features and possibilities, adding diversity to the gameplay.
