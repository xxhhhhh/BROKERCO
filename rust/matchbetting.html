Skin gambling websites enable players to use their skins to place bets on esports matches, and they can wager as many skins as they desire. During the game, the skins that players are staking are removed from their inventory and locked. If they win the bet, they receive back their own skins along with the skins that their opponent gambled and lost, which are placed back in their inventory.
